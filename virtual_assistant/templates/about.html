Aligned

Spend your time on what matters to you.

You know how you want to spend your time — health, relationships, meaningful work. But somehow the day fills up with other things. Aligned fixes that.

What should I do next?

That's the question Aligned answers. You tell it your priorities, and it schedules your time accordingly — writing focused work blocks directly into your calendar, fitted around your existing commitments.

Tell it what matters

You can set two kinds of goals:

  • Time goals — "I want to spend 8 hours a week on fitness"
  • Recurring goals — "Call Joe every 30 days"

Aligned tracks your progress against these goals and schedules your time to keep you on track.

It decides what comes first

Aligned doesn't just shuffle tasks randomly. It prioritises based on what matters most right now: hard deadlines come first, then goals where you're falling behind, then things you haven't done in a while. It also tries to keep you in flow by grouping similar work together.

It adapts to reality

Plans change. Something urgent comes up, or a task takes longer than expected. Traditional planners just mark everything overdue. Aligned reschedules around what actually happened, so your plan always reflects today, not last Monday.

It asks when it's unsure

If Aligned can't figure out the next step for a goal, it'll ask rather than guess. For example: "Your top priority is marathon training, but I'm not sure what to schedule. Should I put in a run, or would you rather plan a training programme first?"

Your calendar stays yours

Aligned writes to a dedicated calendar and never touches your existing events. You'll always see its suggestions as separate blocks alongside your normal schedule.

Simple to use

Tell Aligned what matters to you. Check your calendar to see what to work on next. Let it know when things change. That's it.

Pricing

Aligned is currently free. No credit card needed. If that changes, we'll let you know well in advance.
